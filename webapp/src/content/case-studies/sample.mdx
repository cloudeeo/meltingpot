**Context** — A Series B fintech with 180 engineers and four parallel
delivery streams was experiencing dependency conflicts, missed milestones
and reactive leadership escalations.

**What we did** — Designed a single programme governance layer over the
existing squads, introduced a weekly portfolio cadence and rebuilt the
escalation path so leadership only saw decisions, not status.

**Outcome** — Delivery predictability moved from ~50% to ~85% over the
following quarter, with no headcount changes.
